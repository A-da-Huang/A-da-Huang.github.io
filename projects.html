Tsunami Model

This was a mini scale model showing off how a tsunami was formed for elementary teachers to show students.

I created the design of the model. Our team worked together using a plastic bin to hold the water, we also cut out a hole in the bin so that the water could drain out. The wave was created using a plastic plate, hot glued onto a tube that we would use to pull up and down to create the waves. We had a plastic ramp and a little landscape area where the wave crashed into simulated a tsunami.
